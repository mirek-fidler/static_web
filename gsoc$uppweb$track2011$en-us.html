Track for GSoC 2011 participation

 

We have begun this page in 2010 so we have a year for next application. There is time to do the necessary steps to be chosen to participate. Here we include our working plan, main landmarks and status.

 

Contents

1. 2010 Application Experience. We applied to 2010 without success. Here there are the lessons learned.

2. Purpose. Purpose and guide of next year application and participation.

3. Plan to 2011. Main landmarks to next participation.

4. News

 

Purpose

 

The purpose and guide of this year long effort to U++ project application and participation in GSoC is:

 

To develop a system that:

Guide a person from the very first day in U++ to be the best community developer.

Let this person to get it in a finite deadline.

 

Join project and the community as the same thing.

 

Because GSoC lasts few months, but U++ is opened 365/24:

to give all programmers the best tools

and to receive

more or less skilled people

with more or less time

but feeling that here there is something worth spending their valuable time.
